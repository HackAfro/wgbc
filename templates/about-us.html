our history

Harbour Technologies Limited is an indigenous limited liability company incorporated (RC.502263) and operating in Nigeria, with head office at No. 2A Steve Ihedigbo Street, Ajao Estate Isolo, Lagos. Our services center on Maritime Civil Construction, Foundation works and Engineering Procurement

Our Mission

To deliver quality maritime infrastructural development in our shores and the world over, through world class quality engineering services.
